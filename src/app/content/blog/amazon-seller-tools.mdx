### 7. ACoS Calculator

**Status**: ‚úÖ Active
**Version**: 2.0.0

üîç **Description**: Comprehensive advertising analysis with predictive metrics and historical trend analysis.

**Features**:

- Advanced campaign tracking
- Predictive revenue analysis
- Real-time performance metrics
- Historical trend analysis with visualization
- Enhanced CSV import/export for bulk data
- Customizable reporting periods
- Performance benchmarking against industry standards

**Use Case:**

- Optimize bids based on real-time ACoS data to maximize profitability.

**Limitations:**

- The ACoS Calculator relies heavily on CSV data imports for bulk analysis. Users must ensure that their CSV files adhere to the expected format to avoid processing errors.
- While the tool offers predictive revenue analysis, the accuracy of predictions can vary based on the quality and completeness of input data.
- The ACoS Calculator may not accurately reflect data for campaigns with very low spend.

**Known Bugs:**

- There have been reports of errors during CSV parsing, especially when the CSV contains unexpected or malformed data. Users should verify their CSV format and data integrity before uploading.
- Some users have experienced discrepancies in real-time performance metrics due to data synchronization issues.
